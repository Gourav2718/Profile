2. LIBRARY MANAGEMENT SYSTEM:

Developed a comprehensive Library Management System enabling efficient management of books and materials through automation of lending, returns, and cataloging. Improved user experience with advanced search and reservation features, centralized data access, and real-time catalog updates. 3. GUARDIANS OF MAHARASHTRA: A MAP OF HISTORIC FORTS:

Developed "Guardians of Maharashtra" – an interactive digital platform dedicated to showcasing the architectural and historical significance of Maharashtra's forts. The platform features offline-accessible fort maps, rich historical narratives, curated visual galleries, and practical travel information such as trekking routes and nearby attractions. Enabled user-generated content to foster community engagement and build a collaborative knowledge base for history enthusiasts, travelers, and researchers.
